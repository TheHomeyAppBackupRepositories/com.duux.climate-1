Monitoring and operating your indoor climate was never this easy. Start your road to better air by connecting your favourite Duux products*.

*Please note that this app is only compatible with Edge, Threesixty 2, Bright, Beam 2 and Beam Mini 2 of which the serial number starts with “22”. To enable control via Homey, each product has to be added to the Duux (2022) app for iOS or Android first.
